Government Schemes and Financial Aids

National Livestock Mission (NLM)

Promotes sustainable development in the livestock sector with breed improvement and feed development programs.

  • Financial assistance up to 50% for livestock projects
  • Support for fodder and feed development
  • Infrastructure development assistance

Rashtriya Gokul Mission (RGM)

Focuses on conservation and improvement of indigenous bovine breeds.

  • Breed multiplication farms
  • Scientific breeding programs
  • IVF-based technology support

Dairy Entrepreneurship Development Scheme (DEDS)

Encourages modern dairy farms and milk processing units.

  • Subsidy ranging from 25% to 33%
  • Subsidized loans through NABARD
  • Training and capacity building
